Privacy Policy for Detox & Focus App

Last updated: October 30, 2025

1. Information Collection

Detox & Focus does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. All focus sessions, blocked apps, and reminders are stored locally on your device only.

2. Permissions

The app may request permission to view your app usage data to enable app blocking features. This data is processed locally and is never transmitted or shared externally.

3. Third-Party Services

Detox & Focus does not use any third-party analytics, ads, or tracking tools.

4. Data Security

All app data remains on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data.
